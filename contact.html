Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our platform.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime from the 'Billing' section of your Dashboard. There are no cancellation fees.

Do I need any special equipment?

Not at all. A quiet space and a comfortable place to sit (a chair works perfectly) are all you need. We recommend headphones for the best audio experience.

Are the live sessions recorded?

Yes, all live sessions are recorded and uploaded to the 'Featured Sessions' section of the Portfolio within 24 hours for Premium members.
