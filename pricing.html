Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Serenity memberships and billing.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any point from your account dashboard. You'll retain all membership benefits until the end of your current billing period.

Do you offer a student or educator discount?

Yes we do! We're proud to support the educational community with 30% off our Annual Plan. Simply email us at hello@serenitystudio.com with your institution credentials to get started.

Can I upgrade from Monthly to Annual later?

Of course. You can switch plans at any time through your settings. When you upgrade, we'll pro-rate your remaining monthly balance and apply it to your new annual subscription.

What happens after my 14-day free trial?

If you choose not to cancel, your membership will automatically transition to your selected paid plan. We'll send you a courtesy reminder email 48 hours before your trial expires.
